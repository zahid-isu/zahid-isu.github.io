About Me

I am a Graduate Research Assistant at the Self-aware Complex System Lab in Iowa State University. I am working with Dr. Soumik Sarkar on computer vision for autonomous driving and video action understanding. I am pursuing my Ph.D. in Electrical Engineering at the Electrical and Computer Engineering Department, under the supervision of Dr. Soumik Sarkar and Dr. Baskar Ganapathysubramanian. For my current project, I am working on finetuning Vision-language Foundation models (VLMs), investigating the robustness of VLMs, edge-case generation for Foundation models, understanding human actions in video using VLMs and so on. I have experience in working with multimodal (image, video, text) and multi-source (transportation data, clinical data, video data, etc.) data to finetune foundation models in Computer Vision.

Research

My research interests are in the intersection of Computer Vision (CV), Foundation Models (VLM/MLLM), Multi-modal learning, Autonomous Driving (AD), and Artificial Intelligence (AI) models robustness.
